A Chelsea Boot Built to Endure

There are shoes bought for the season, and shoes that outlast a wardrobe. The Brecon Chelsea Boot was designed with the latter in mind — a considered piece built on over 100 years of British design heritage, and priced accordingly at £260.

Premium polished calf leather forms the upper, paired with a full leather lining and suede heel counter that shapes to the foot over time. Beneath it all, a density impact studded rubber sole and storm welt provide grip and durability — built equally for city pavements and weekend lanes.

The Brecon works as well with a suit as it does with weekend denim. It is, in the truest sense, a boot designed not for the moment but for the years ahead — the kind of piece that earns its place in a wardrobe and simply stays there.
